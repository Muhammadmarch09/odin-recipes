Carrot salad

This carrot salad is quick and easy to make with just five ingredients. My grandma made it all the time, and I loved it. Now I make it to honor her, and it's almost as good.I put some crushed pineapple in mine delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shredded carrots
  • 1 ¼ cups raisins
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Mix shredded carrots and raisins together in a large salad bowl.
  3. Whisk mayonnaise, lemon juice, and salt in a small bowl until smooth.
  4. Pour over carrot mixture; stir until carrots and raisins are completely coated. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
